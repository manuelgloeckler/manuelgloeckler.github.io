# Theorems and Proofs

I created a nicely looking theorem, lemma, definition and proof classes.

[def]
A set $C$ is *convex* if for all
$x,y \in C$ and for all
$\alpha \in [0,1]$ the point
$\alpha x + (1-\alpha) y \in C$.
[/def]
